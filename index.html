About Us

People have been using herbal remedies for countless centuries and we want the healing, the wisdom of ancient generation to reach across the ages. A thought that guides everything we create. From that thought, RUKMA products was born. RUKMA is the new organic - healthy choice in Ventha Velichenna and Danthapala oil. Ventha Velichenna is the traditional version of the Hot-Processed Virgin Coconut Oil. It is a recipe that’s been passed on through generations in Kerala households. This handmade oil is pure, unadulterated and is extracted from finest - organic coconuts. From cooking to skincare, this oil is a versatile addition to any household. Every mother has a secret home remedy that actually works. Danthapala oil is such a sacred remedy that help us to fight dandruff and other skin conditions caused by fungal infection. Danthapala oil is the purest blend of danthapala leaves with virgin coconut oil, traditionally extracted and is prepared in sunlight. Danthapala oil can provide a lot of benefits with everyday use.
